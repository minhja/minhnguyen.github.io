BMFTR-MOST (DE-IL) Grant Awarded

05 June 2026

Pleased to share that I have officially achieved my first (big) competitive grant titled Enhancing Boron and Micropollutant Removal from Water by Molecular Layer Deposition Membranes (EMPOWERED). This is a BMFTR-MOST collaborative grant between Germany (Prof. Andrea I. Schäfer and I, KIT) and Israel (Prof. Tamar Segal-Peretz, Technion).

I received the announcement that the project was funded from the Israeli side in December 2025. However, the German side still required some technical clarification and a lot of administrative tasks, and BMFTR just issued the formal funding notice yesterday (after 6 months!). The project will start on 1st July 2026. I will serve as project coordinator and my salary will be covered by this grant for two and a half years. This grant also subsidises a PhD student from Technion to conduct research at KIT for 6 months. So far so good!

Abbreviations: BMFTR – Federal Ministry of Research, Technology and Space of Germany; MOST – Ministry of Innovation, Science and Technology of Israel.
