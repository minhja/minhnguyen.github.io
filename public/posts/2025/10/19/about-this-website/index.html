About This Website

19 October 2025

This is my personal website, where I feature my research directions, publications, and CV.

I also set up a blog where I share my achievements (paper publications, awarded grants, etc.), some thoughts on my academic journey, and other stuff.

The design of this website

The website is simplistic and fit for its simple purposes. I keep the main sections (Research, Publications, and CV) updated from time to time. Blog posts are time-stamped and organised based on tags and categories. No search bar, because I think it is a distraction.

Due to privacy concerns, I do not track visits or enable comments using tools such as Google Analytics or Disqus. If needs be in the future, I may opt for some privacy-focused alternatives.

If you want to follow my posts, please subscribe to the website’s RSS (which is an archaic content format) at the bottom of each page, next to the copyright information.

The website is hosted by Github and built from Hugo. The website layouts are adapted from the Hugo-Xmin theme by Yihui Xie, with some slight tuning of fonts and colours.

What you will NOT find on this website

  • Unpublished scientific contents in any form (manuscript sections, internal reports, discussions, ideas, etc.);
  • Donation requests;
  • My political views (I detest discussing politics in scientific settings).
