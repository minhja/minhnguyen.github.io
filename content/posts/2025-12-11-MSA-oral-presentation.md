Pleased to share that I received the Membrane Society of Australasia (MSA) Best Oral Presentation Award at the IMSTEC 2025 Conference, Gold Coast, Australia.

My presentation titled **Collisions between singlet oxygen and steroid hormone micropollutants in photocatalytic membrane pores** was based on a recently published paper in Chemical Engineering Journal ([link here](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cej.2024.157582), 2024 IF of 13.2).

In the presentation, I discussed how the century-old [**collision theory**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Collision_theory) could be adapted to predict the rate of micropollutant removal by photocatalytic membrane reactors.
